Overall: VERY GOOD

What a beautiful and cute hidden gem in Montreal! The ceviche was fresh and delicious. I tried out the mango flavor, medium spiced. My friend and I also had the chicken tacos and we were served 4 different sauces to try out with them. The latté I had was nice too. Overall a little taste of the Caribbean, where you will feel transported away in the setting. Will definitely go back to try out some other menu items! Everything on it looks delicious.
